Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI consulting services.

What types of AI solutions do you develop?

We specialize in custom machine learning models, natural language processing, computer vision, predictive analytics, automation systems, and AI strategy consulting. Our solutions are tailored to your specific business needs and industry requirements.

How long does a typical AI project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple automation projects may take 4-8 weeks, while comprehensive AI systems can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you work with small businesses or just enterprises?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies. Our approach is scalable and we tailor our solutions to fit your budget and growth objectives. Every project gets the same level of expertise and attention to detail.

What data do you need to get started?

The data requirements depend on your specific project goals. We can work with structured data (databases, spreadsheets), unstructured data (text, images, audio), or help you develop a data collection strategy. We'll assess your current data landscape during our consultation.

How do you ensure data security and privacy?

Data security is our top priority. We implement end-to-end encryption, secure data processing pipelines, and strict access controls. We're compliant with industry standards like GDPR and CCPA, and can work within your existing security frameworks.

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including monitoring, maintenance, model retraining, and system optimization. AI systems require ongoing attention to maintain peak performance, and we're here to ensure your investment continues to deliver value.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, retainer agreements, and equity partnerships for startups. Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Can you integrate AI with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in seamless integration with existing infrastructure. Whether you're using cloud platforms, on-premise systems, or hybrid environments, we design AI solutions that work with your current technology stack without disruption.
